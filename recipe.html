Beef Chili


Information

This spanish dish is good served with cheese and a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • 2tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1/2lb. Ground beef
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Kidney beans
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Cheddar
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Heat oil at medium heat in a pot
  2. Add onion and cook until soft (5 minutes)
  3. Add tomato paste and stirr
  4. Add ground beef and cook until no longer pink
  5. Add chili powder, cumin, oregano and paprika
  6. Season with salt & pepper
  7. Pour kidney beans and crushed tomatoes
  8. Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes
  9. Ladle into bowls and enjoy!
